This Year's Entertainment

This year, we have many featured performers. All with their own unique talents!
Live shows and performance just for your entertainment.
All the performance’s will be on the main stage every day at 1pm, 3pm, and 5pm. Be sure to tip your entertainers if you enjoy their performance!

Vancouver Ballet

Vancouver Ballet’s demo team here with their own unique style and choreography. Vancouver Ballet is known for their touring team where they perform worldwide and attend competitions.

Alex the firebreather

Alex the firebreather is a one man show who will surely blow you away. From fire hula hoops to fire swords, Alex’s skills in fire manipulation will truly have you question if the fire has a life of its own.

Nikolay’s Magic Show

Nikolay the world famous magician who won first place at the Canadian’s best magicians will be giving you a performance of a lifetime. Make sure you don’t miss Nikolay’s winning act!

Punky Band

Canada’s #1 rated band is here to perform just for you. Performing their hit single, Lets go to the mall. Make sure you give them a visit and you can find more information here.

Green Dance

A mix between a dance and a play. This talented group will present their beautiful storytelling through music and movement that will have you in awe. Making you question how you can understand the emotional story from beginning to end without hearing a single word.
