Welcome to the Westcoast Food Truck Festival

The West Coast Food Truck Festival (WCFTF) is back in beautiful Stanley Park for its 6th year!
This year we will also be open three days instead of two. In addition to our regular Saturday and Sunday 10am – 10pm hours, for the first time, we will be open on Friday from 5pm – 10pm so you can come on down after work and school to start the weekend off right!
As always, The WCFTC features trucks with food from all around the world, as well as a variety of live entertainment. Your favorites are all back:
Juanita’s Fajitas, Burger Shake and the Little Café. Glow’s coffee and Donuts will also be back, and those who attended last year will know to arrive early and get in line to get in on their magical donuts before they sell out! We have also expanded our festival space to make room for new additions such as Connor’s creamery, Bloch, Extreme Coffee and many more.
We have a wide range of performers from musicians, dancers, magicians and live cooking demonstrations. Entertainment will run on the main stage for the duration of the festival so feel free to sit back, relax, and watch while you enjoy your meals.
Admission is free and all tips go directly to the vendors and entertainment.
