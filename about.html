The Greater Vancouver Food Truck Festival is dedicated to accommodating out guests in the following ways:

  • Accessible parking located near the festival entrance
  • Designated wheelchair areas in the audience with clear stage visibility
  • Wheelchair accessible washrooms
  • Detachable card readers to facilitate credit/debit payment
  • Festival attendants will be available to answer questions and help with accessibility
If you have any questions or concerns about accessibility at the festival, please email contact@wcftf.ca and we’d be happy to offer our support!

All our performance are kid friendly! We also have amazing options for kids such as Connor’s Creamery’s blue drizzle cones or Fish & Chips kids size meal featuring cod or halibut.
You also can not go wrong with Burger Shake, which also has a kids menu, burgers, fries and shakes that no kid can resist.
